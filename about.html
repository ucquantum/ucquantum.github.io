About Us

Workshops

Join us for our weekly meetings in which we discuss everything between the physics that governs the quantum world to the algorithms that allow computation.

We meet every Wednesday from 6:15-7:30. These meetings are lead by students and often do not require much previous experience to join in on the discussion.

Lab Tours/ Experiments

Throughout the year, we will host lab tours of prominent UChicago faculty on campus as well as Argonne national laboratory, and conduct experiments to demostrate quantum properties.

To learn more about previous events and how to stay informed, check out our Events page.

Speaker Events

UC Quantum hosts two speaker events each quarter where we invite field experts to lecture and anybody interested to attend. Catering will be provided. Attendance at speaker events is also used to gauging interest in certain topics or applications of quantum computing.

UChicago is uniquely situated to be part of the Chicago Quantum Exchange which allows us to explore a wide variety of research areas.

Community

UC Quantum aims to bring together students of all backgrounds to learn about and discuss contemporary questions that are broadly related to quantum computing. Each student, regardless of their knowledge base, is encouraged to attend or lead our weekly meetings, where we focus on learning about the fundamental concepts necessary to understanding the quantum computing realm.

We are always eager to welcome new members to our community. Please check out our Facebook page and Slack channel. Both will be used to facilitate active discussion of our current interests.
