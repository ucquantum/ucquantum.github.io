About Us

Student Workshops

Join us for our weekly meetings in which we discuss everything between the physics that governs the quantum world to the algorithms that allow computation.

We meet every Wednesday from 6:15-7:30. These meetings are lead by students and often do not need much previous experience to join in on the discussion.

Lab Tours/ Experiments

Throughout the year, we will host lab tours of prominent UChicago faculty on campus as well as Argonne national laboratory, and conduct experiments demonstrating quantum properties.

To learn more about previous events and how to stay informed, check out our Events page.

Invited Guest Speakers

The current plan is to host two faculty speakers a quarter, these events will be catered and provide possible guidance for proceeding events.

UChicago is uniquely situated to be part of the Chicago Quantum Exchange which allows us to explore a wide variety of research areas.

Amazing Community!

We are a student run group which allows us to determine exactly what we want to be! All students of any background and any amount of knowledge of quantum computing are welcome in this space. There are many opportunities for student leadership available, please just reach out if you have an idea or interest for the club!

We are constantly looking for new students to join our group. Please check out our Facebook page and Slack channel. Both will be used to encourage active discussion of our current interests.
